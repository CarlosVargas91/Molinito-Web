Nuestra Historia

From the mill,
to your table

El Molinito takes its name from the small stone mill that has ground corn into masa for centuries across Mexico. That same grinding stone — patient, precise, transformative — is at the heart of everything we do.

We bring authentic Mexican flavors to events across the Netherlands. From intimate dinners to large corporate gatherings, our chefs prepare each dish with the same care and tradition passed down through generations.
